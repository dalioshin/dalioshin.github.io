VIDEO ABOUT PORTFOLIO CONTACT

Desmond Alioshin

I seek to not limit my creativity and skills to one domain. I am an observer, learner, and maker.

I have 4 years of experience working as a software and machine learning engineer at Docusign. While there I learned valuable lessons about building scalable and reliable software. Since leaving I have been focused on my art absorbing as much about the stars and universe as I can, and then trying to squeeze those ideas into different mediums. The goal is to share that wonderful feeling of exploration and discovery with as many people as I can reach. Before my industry experience I attended Rensselear Polytechnic Institute and graduated with a BS in Computer and Systems Engineering. And before my university experience I lived as a child with many great teachers, official and not, who encouraged my creative spirit.
